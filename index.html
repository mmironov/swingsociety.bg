Solo Jazz - All levels

In this class we explore solo steps, routines and rhythms, typical for the Vernacular Jazz Dances in the 1920 - 1950 period.

No prior experience is needed.

Join at any time by showing up at the studio (Serdika Str 25).

Lindy Hop - Intermediate

In this class we dive in the details of Lindy Hop, improve on the basics, work on technique and jazz vocabulary and start to explore different rhythmical variations.

You need at least 3 months of experience in order to join this class.

Join at any time by showing up at the studio (Serdika Str 25).

Lindy Hop - Old Clips Inspiration

If you are serious about the Lindy Hop and the Jazz dances, this is the group for you. Here we work on rhythms, moves and routines based on the old dance clips of the 1920 - 1950 period. In this group we also explore the extremes - very slow and very fast tempos.

You need at least 1 year of experience in order to join this class.

Join at any time by showing up at the studio (Serdika Str 25).
